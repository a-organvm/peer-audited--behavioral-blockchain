## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary: Relationship Coaches
- Solo practitioners or small practices (1-5 coaches)
- Specialize in breakup recovery, divorce coaching, attachment healing
- Charge $90-$300/hr, manage 10-25 active clients
- Pain: limited visibility into client behavior between sessions, reliance on self-reporting
- Gain: 24/7 automated compliance monitoring, quantified outcomes, premium package upsell

### Secondary: Licensed Therapists (LMFT, LCSW, LPC)
- Work with relationship/attachment issues
- More cautious about technology adoption (HIPAA concerns)
- Higher willingness to pay if compliance/privacy requirements met
- Pain: administrative burden, session-only insight into client progress

### Tertiary: Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOPs) / Clinics
- Enterprise tier ($999+/mo)
- Multi-provider, need supervisor views and aggregate analytics
- Require white-labeling, HIPAA BAA, data export
