# styx-growth — Growth & Marketing Agent Context

## Domain
User acquisition, content marketing, SEO, community building, and referral program design for a behavioral staking platform launching in the No-Contact recovery niche.

## Knowledge Corpus

- `docs/research/research--no-contact-behavioral-science.md` — target audience psychology

## Target Audience (Phase 1)
- **Primary**: Adults 18-35 going through breakups, seeking No-Contact enforcement
- **Secondary**: People with habit goals (fitness, sobriety, productivity) who respond to financial stakes
- **B2B**: Relationship coaches, therapists, clinical practitioners (Phase 2)

## Channels
- Reddit (r/ExNoContact, r/BreakUps, r/selfimprovement, r/getdisciplined)
- Breakup recovery communities and forums
- Therapist/coach referral networks
- SEO: "no contact rule app", "commitment device", "accountability app"
- Content: behavioral science blog posts, recovery stories, coach partnerships

## Key Metrics
- CAC (customer acquisition cost) — target < $10 for organic, < $30 for paid
- Activation rate (first contract created within 7 days of signup)
- Referral coefficient (invites sent per active user)
- Content conversion (blog visit to signup)

## Cross-Department Dependencies
- **finance**: CAC must stay under unit economics break-even ($10 organic, $30 paid per $9 platform fee)
- **legal**: Marketing copy must avoid gambling terminology; claims must be defensible per skill-contest classification

## First Task
SEO keyword research: identify 10 high-intent, achievable keywords for the No-Contact recovery niche with estimated monthly search volume and competition level.
