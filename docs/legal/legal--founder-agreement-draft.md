# **Founder Agreement (MVP Phase)**

**Date:** \[Insert Date\]

This Founder Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**Founder 1:** Jessica \[Last Name\]\
**Founder 2:** \[Partner Full Name\]

Collectively referred to as the "Founders."

# **1. Purpose**

The Founders agree to collaborate on the development, testing, and
launch of a behavioral change product currently referred to as the
**Behavioral Change System MVP** (the "Product").

The Product may include:

- Behavioral enforcement systems

- Software tools and platforms

- Community-based accountability systems

- Branding, content, and intellectual property related to the Product

This agreement governs the collaboration during the **MVP testing phase
prior to formal LLC formation.**

# **2. Ownership**

All intellectual property created in connection with the Product during
the MVP phase shall be jointly owned by the Founders.

**Equity Split**

Founder 1 (Jessica): **50%\**
Founder 2: **50%**

This includes but is not limited to:

- Software code

- Product systems

- Branding

- Documentation

- Product concepts

- Data models

- Marketing assets

Neither Founder may claim sole ownership of the Product or associated
intellectual property.

Upon formation of a future LLC, **all intellectual property created
during the MVP phase will be assigned to the LLC.**

# **3. Roles and Responsibilities**

### **Founder 1 (Jessica)**

Responsible for:

- Audience distribution and marketing

- Brand positioning

- Community leadership and program delivery

- Product vision and behavioral framework

- Operational oversight of MVP cohorts

### **Founder 2**

Responsible for:

- Technical development

- System architecture

- Infrastructure and platform integration

- Data systems and analytics support

Major strategic decisions regarding the Product must be agreed upon by
both Founders.

# **4. Revenue During MVP Phase**

Revenue generated during the MVP phase (such as entry fees from
participants) will be split according to the agreed ownership
percentages.

Founder 1: **50%\**
Founder 2: **50%**

Participant **deposits are not revenue** and will be treated as funds
held for redistribution according to the program rules.

# **5. Expenses**

Business expenses incurred during the MVP phase must be documented.

Any expense above **\$250** should be approved by both Founders before
being incurred.

Approved expenses will be deducted before profit distribution.

# **6. Formation of Future LLC**

If the MVP proves successful, the Founders agree to form a new Limited
Liability Company (LLC).

Upon formation:

- All intellectual property created during the MVP phase will be
  transferred into the LLC.

- Ownership of the LLC will reflect the agreed equity split unless
  otherwise renegotiated.

# **7. Founder Departure**

If either Founder leaves the project before the LLC is formed:

- Ownership of intellectual property created during the MVP phase will
  be determined based on contribution and prior agreement.

- Neither Founder may independently commercialize the Product using
  jointly developed intellectual property.

# **8. Non-Circumvention**

Neither Founder may independently develop or launch a competing version
of the Product using the jointly developed concept, systems, or
intellectual property without written agreement from the other Founder.
