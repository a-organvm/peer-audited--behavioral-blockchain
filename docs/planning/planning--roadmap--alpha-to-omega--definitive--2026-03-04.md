# Definitive Alpha→Omega Roadmap (March 4, 2026 Baseline)

## Roadmap Promise
This is the locked execution sequence from current state to the Omega outcome: a legally durable, trust-first behavioral commitment platform with enterprise-scale revenue lanes.

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- Keep the product framed as a skill-based behavioral commitment system.
- Keep Phase 1 focused on iOS-first, no-contact recovery-first execution.
- Do not introduce shared-pot or chance-like mechanics before legal gates are complete.
- Do not scale money movement until jurisdiction, settlement, and identity controls are live.
- Every phase exit requires evidence, not opinion.

## Current Position (March 4, 2026)
- Foundation digestion is complete: all tracked source documents are ingested and mapped.
- Core product loop exists and is testable.
- Compliance and trust controls are partially in place.
- Remaining high-priority gaps are now converted into executable ticket bundles.

## Scope Guardrails (What We Explicitly Defer)
- Shared-pot and PvP prize mechanics remain post-gate and legally isolated.
- Experimental moonshot verification layers remain research-only until core trust economics are stable.
- New category expansion does not preempt the locked Beta->Gamma->Delta critical path.

## Success Definition
Success is reaching Omega with a platform that is:
- legally durable,
- trusted by users and reviewers,
- measurable in outcomes,
- and monetizable in both consumer and enterprise lanes.
