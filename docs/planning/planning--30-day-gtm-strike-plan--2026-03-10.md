# 30-Day GTM Strike Plan

This is the first execution map for making the Phase 1 beta live in the market. It assumes the current public wedge remains:

- `No-Contact recovery`
- `iOS` private beta
- `Test-Money Pilot`
- `US allowlist`

It does not assume broad paid acquisition, real-money activation, or generic habit-app marketing.

## Objective

By Day 30, Styx should have:

- one live no-contact landing page
- one live private-beta waitlist flow
- one emergency-moment asset
- at least 4 published high-intent content assets
- one creator outreach motion running
- one practitioner outreach motion running
- one small curated beta cohort admitted
- one weekly reporting rhythm for channel performance

## Owner Legend

- `H:FO` = founder / product lead
- `H:BD` = Jessica / business development / partnerships

## Target Metrics For Day 30

These are not vanity goals. They are enough to judge whether the wedge is real.

- `100+` waitlist signups from qualified sources
- `40%+` waitlist-to-qualified rate
- `10+` creator replies
- `5+` creator conversations
- `10+` practitioner replies
- `5+` practitioner conversations
- `15+` admitted beta users in the first curated cohort
- top 2 channels identified by conversion quality, not traffic

## Core Rules

1. Every public asset must point to one CTA:
   `Join the Private Beta`
2. Do not broaden the positioning during the first 30 days.
3. Do not spend on paid acquisition yet.
4. Do not lead with `blockchain`, `Fury`, or `real-money` language.
5. Track source quality, not just signup count.
6. Treat creator and practitioner conversations as market research, not just distribution.
