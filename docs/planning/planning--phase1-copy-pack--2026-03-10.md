## 2. Homepage Supporting Sections

### Problem Section

Header:

- `Breakups do not fail because people do not know what to do.`

Body:

- `They fail because the hardest moment is not the plan. It is 11:47 PM, loneliness, one urge, and no structure strong enough to hold. Styx is built for that moment.`

### How It Works Section

Header:

- `A stronger structure for no-contact recovery.`

Steps:

1. `Set the commitment.`  
   Choose your no-contact contract and define the recovery period you are trying to protect.
2. `Check in daily.`  
   Use daily attestation to keep the recovery window visible and active.
3. `Add accountability.`  
   Bring in clear structure, partner support, and consequences that make the next urge harder to rationalize.
4. `Protect the streak that matters.`  
   Not an app streak. Your actual no-contact recovery streak.
