## Content Production Guidelines

### Blog Posts
- **Length:** 800-1500 words
- **SEO:** Target 1-2 keywords per post, include in title, H2, and first paragraph
- **CTA:** Every post ends with a relevant CTA (sign up, join beta, become a Fury, book a demo)
- **Imagery:** Custom header graphic, 1-2 inline diagrams/charts
- **Internal linking:** Link to related Styx blog posts and product pages

### Social Posts
- **Twitter/X:** Threads for educational content, single tweets for stats/quotes, always end threads with CTA
- **LinkedIn:** Professional tone, practitioner-focused, use carousels for visual content
- **Hashtags:** #accountability #behavioralscience #lossaversion #habitchange #recoverytools

### Newsletter
- **Sending day:** Friday 10am ET
- **Format:** 1 main story (400-600 words) + 3 quick links + 1 CTA
- **Subject line formula:** "[Topic] — [Curiosity hook]" (e.g., "The $39 Experiment — Would you risk it?")
- **Segmentation:** Consumer vs. Practitioner lists (diverge at Week 5)

### Discord
- **#announcements:** Product updates, new features, launch milestones
- **#science:** Behavioral science discussion, research papers, λ=1.955 deep dives
- **#recovery:** Moderated support channel for Recovery oath participants
- **#furies:** Auditor coordination, training materials, audit tips
