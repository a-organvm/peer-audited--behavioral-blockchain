# Hiring & Team Building

Job descriptions, interview processes, and team growth planning for Styx.

## What Lives Here

- **Hiring timeline** — Three scenarios (conservative, moderate, aggressive) for team growth
- **Job descriptions** — Role definitions for each position we plan to hire
- **Interview guides** — What to look for, questions to ask, evaluation criteria
- **Contractor vetting** — How to find, evaluate, and manage contract workers
- **Onboarding checklist** — What new team members need on day one

## Who Uses This

- **Non-technical co-founder**: You'll lead most hiring conversations. These docs help you explain roles and evaluate candidates.
- **Technical co-founder**: Technical interview support, defining engineering role requirements
