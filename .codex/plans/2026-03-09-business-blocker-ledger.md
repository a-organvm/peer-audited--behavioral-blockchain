Date: 2026-03-09

### Objective

Produce a partner-facing blocker ledger that strips away speculative planning and presents only the concrete non-engineering gates that can stop Beta progress.

### Deliverable

Create `docs/planning/planning--business-blocker-ledger--2026-03-09.md` with:

1. A definition of what counts as an actual blocker.
2. The current concrete business/legal/ops blockers.
3. The next human action for each blocker.
4. The evidence required to mark each blocker complete.
5. A short list of items that matter later but do not currently block Beta.

### Scope

Focus on partner-owned or shared human blockers:

- `#132` KYC provider + DPA
- `#133` merchant / processor underwriting
- `#136` skill-based contest whitepaper + counsel sign-off
- `#141` App Store Connect + TestFlight provisioning
